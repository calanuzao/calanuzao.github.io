Understanding Complex Numbers in DSP

Complex numbers are fundamental in digital signal processing because they allow us to represent and manipulate signals in both time and frequency domains. The unit circle provides a geometric interpretation of complex numbers and helps visualize concepts like phase and magnitude.

In DSP, complex numbers are used to:

  • Represent signals with both magnitude and phase information
  • Perform frequency analysis using the Fourier Transform
  • Design and implement digital filters
  • Analyze and process signals in the complex plane
